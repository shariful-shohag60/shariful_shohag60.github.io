Research Interests

Projects

  • DRAM-based Latch-type Sense Amplifier: Designed and simulated a sense amplifier using LTspice circuit simulator, achieving improved signal integrity and reduced power consumption.
  • Phase Change Memory (PCM) Simulation: Simulated the reset operation of a mushroom cell structure in a Ge2Sb2Te5-based PCM nano-device using COMSOL Multiphysics, optimizing performance for high-density memory applications.
  • Obstacle-Avoiding Robotic Car: Implemented and simulated the hardware circuitry of a robotic car in Proteus circuit simulator, enabling autonomous navigation with obstacle detection.
